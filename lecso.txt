Ingredients (lecso total: 2,071 kcal, 1900g, 109 kcal per 100g)
    1 kg diced onions
        410 kcal
    4 tsp garlic
        20 kcal
    2 kg peppers (hungarian, cubanelle, bell peppers)
        480 kcal
    1 kg tomatoes
        170 kcal
    1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
        484 kcal
    3 tbsp paprika
        57 kcal
    2 cups cooked chickpeas
        450 kcal
    4 cups rice
        3300 kcal

Instructions
    Dice onions

    In large pot, heat olive oil (6 heat)
    Add onions and saute until golden brown (20 minutes)
    Add garlic and saute (1 minute)

    Wash and dice peppers
    Dice tomatoes

    Stir in paprika, peppers, and tomatoes
    Bring to boil
    Cook uncovered (5 heat) (10 minutes)
    Stir in 4 tsp salt and 20 turns pepper
    Cook covered (4 heat) (60 minutes)
    Cook uncovered until reduced (4 heat)
    Add chickpeas and combine

    Cook rice separately, then serve together
