Ingredients
    ~560g dry chickpeas (about 4 tatung cups)
    12 lemons
    12 cloves garlic
    2 cups stirred tahini
    1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
    1 tsp ground cumin
    3 tsp salt

Instructions
    Soak chickpeas overnight

    Rinse chickpeas thoroughly and cook in pressure cooker for 2 hours

    Juice lemons
    Mince/chop/whatever garlic

    Stir until combined
        juice from lemon
        garlic
        cumin
        salt
        tahini
        olive oil
        a bit of water
    Blend/process/whatever until desired consistency
        chickpeas
        a bunch of water
            If refrigeration is desired, add too much water, then even more on top of that
