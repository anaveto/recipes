Ingredients
    1/8 cup olive oil
    0.5 kg chopped onions
    1.5 tbsp chopped garlic
    0.5 kg dry red split lentils
    1.5 kg frozen stuff as desired (potatoes, carrots, peas, corn, tomatoes, etc)
    1.5 tbsp paprika
    2 tsp bouillon
    0.5 tsp dried thyme
    1 tsp sugar
    0.5 tbsp lemon juice
    2 bay leaves

Instructions
    Rinse and drain lentils

    In large pot, heat olive oil (160c)
    Add onions and cook uncovered until lightly browned (160c, 40 minutes)
    Stir in garlic
    Saute for 2 minutes (160c)

    Add lentils, frozen stuff
    Bring to boil covered (max heat)
    Add 5 cups water, 2 tsp salt, 10 turns pepper, paprika, bouillon, thyme, sugar, lemon juice, bay leaves
    Reduce heat and simmer covered (120c, 60 minutes)
