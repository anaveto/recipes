Ingredients
    1/4 cup olive oil
    1 kg chopped onions
    3 tbsp chopped garlic
    1kg dry red split lentils
    3 kg frozen stuff as desired (potatoes, carrots, peas, corn, tomatoes, etc)
    3 tbsp paprika
    4 tsp bouillon
    1 tsp dried thyme
    2 tsp sugar
    1 tbsp lemon juice
    4 bay leaves

Instructions
    Rinse and drain lentils

    In large pot, heat olive oil (160c)
    Add onions and cook uncovered until lightly browned (160c, 40 minutes)
    Stir in garlic
    Saute for 2 minutes (160c)

    Add lentils, frozen stuff
    Bring to boil covered (max heat)
    Add 10 cups water, 4 tsp salt, 20 turns pepper, paprika, bouillon, thyme, sugar, lemon juice, bay leaves
    Reduce heat and simmer covered (120c, 60 minutes)
