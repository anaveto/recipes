Ingredients
    1 kg chopped onions
    8 tsp chopped garlic
    1/2 cup olive oil
    1kg dry brown lentils
    2 kg frozen mixed veggies (peas, carrots, green beans, corn)
    500 g diced tomatoes
    4 tbsp paprika
    4 tsp bouillon
    1 tsp dried thyme
    2 tsp sugar
    1 tbsp lemon juice
    4 bay leaves

Instructions
    Rinse and drain lentils

    In large pot, heat olive oil (160c)
    Add onions and cook uncovered until lightly browned (160c, 20 minutes)
    Stir in garlic
    Saute for 2 minutes (160c)

    Add lentils, veggies, tomatoes
    Add 10 cups water, 6 tsp salt, 20 turns pepper, paprika, bouillon, thyme, sugar, lemon juice, bay leaves
    Bring to boil covered (max heat)
    Reduce heat and simmer covered (110c, 60 minutes)
