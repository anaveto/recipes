Ingredients
    2 cups flour
    1 tsp baking powder
    3/4 tsp baking soda
    1/2 tsp salt
    1 + 1/4 cups chocolate chips
    1/2 cup packed brown sugar
    1/2 cup sugar
    1/2 cup + 1 tbsp avocado oil
    1/4 cup + 1 tbsp water

Instructions
    Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt with whisk
    Add chocolate chips and toss to coat

    In a separate bowl, combine coconut sugar, regular sugar, oil, and water
    Whisk until sugar has incorporated into the oil and mixture is smooth (2 min)

    Add flour mixture to sugar mixture
    Stir until combined and no more flour is visible, but no more than that

    Line 2 baking sheets and portion out cookies (2 inch mounds)
    Freeze on pans for 30 minutes or chill for 24 hours

    Preheat oven to 180 degrees
    Bake until edges starting to turn golden (14-17 min)
    Cool
