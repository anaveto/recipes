Ingredients
    120g flour
    1/2 tsp baking powder
    3/8 tsp baking soda
    1/4 tsp salt
    5/8 cups chocolate chips
    1/3 cup coconut sugar (or 1/4 cup packed brown)
    1/4 cup sugar
    4.5 tbsp avocado oil
    2.5 tbsp water

Instructions
    Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt with whisk
    Add chocolate chips and toss to coat

    In a separate bowl, combine brown sugar, regular sugar, oil, and water
    Whisk until sugar has incorporated into the oil and mixture is smooth (2 min)

    Add flour mixture to sugar mixture
    Stir until combined and no more flour is visible, but no more than that

    Line baking sheet and portion out cookies evenly (~1/4 cup per cookie)
    Put in fridge uncovered for 24 hours (or freezer for 30 minutes)

    Preheat oven to 180 degrees
    Bake until edges starting to turn golden (17 min)
        Flip tray halfway through
    Leave on baking sheet for 15 minutes
    Transfer to cooling rack and cool for at least 5 minutes
